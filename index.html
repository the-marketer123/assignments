New Years Travelogue in Tokyo

My trip to the New Years in Tokyo, Jan 7, 2025

Twas the night before new years, when we arrived in Japan
We smelled the Takoyaki, cooking in the restaurants pan
We play some Karuta, a traditional card game
We turn on the TV, with no fireworks to be seen
I win the last round, enjoying my five minutes of fame
We scatter some Kadomatsu, setting the traditional scene
Made of bamboo and pine, held in a wooden frame

Together we ate our Osechi ryori, a symbolic dish
We wish for success in life, as represented by the fish
We watched the Hatsunohide, the New Year is born
The first sunrise of the year, meaning good fortune
We met with the family, giving gifts adorn
v We enjoyed a family meal, with great proportion
We visited a local shrine, later the same morn
